Add the `sockopt::RcvMark` socket option (Linux/Android, `SO_RCVMARK`,
Linux 5.19+) and decode the resulting `(SOL_SOCKET, SO_MARK)` ancillary
message as a new `ControlMessageOwned::SoMark(u32)` variant. Previously
the cmsg fell through to the catch-all `Unknown` arm, which forced a
per-recv `Vec<u8>` allocation for four bytes.
